OfficeToday · 11:42 AMHigh
The front-office printer stopped answering at 11:42 AM. It had been printing normally all morning, so it most likely lost power, ran out of paper, or had a cable knocked loose. No one in the office can print until it’s back. Try checking that it’s switched on and connected, then print a test page — if that doesn’t help, IT can take a closer look.
Someone tried a wrong password several times
A person typed the wrong password 4 times in a row in Lab 2. This could be someone who forgot it — or someone who shouldn’t be signing in.
Lab 2Today · 10:15 AMMedium
At 10:15 AM someone entered the wrong password four times in a row on a computer in Lab 2. Usually this is just a student or teacher who forgot their password or left caps lock on. Once in a while it can mean someone is trying to get into an account that isn’t theirs. It’s worth asking who was using that computer mid-morning, and offering a password reset if they were simply locked out.
